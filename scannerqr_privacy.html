Privacy Policy

1. General Information

Kiro QR Scan is committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) (EU) 2016/679.

The application is designed to function entirely on the user’s device.

We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell personal data.

2. Data Collection

2.1 Personal Data

Kiro QR Scan does not collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Device identifiers
  • Advertising ID
  • Analytics data

No user account is required.

2.2 Camera Permission

The app requests access to the device camera exclusively for scanning QR codes.

  • Camera data is processed in real time
  • No images or videos are stored
  • No camera data is transmitted externally

All scanning operations occur locally on the device.

2.3 QR Code Content

Scan QR code data:

  • Is processed locally
  • Is not transmitted to any server
  • Is not stored outside the device

If scan history is available, it is stored only on the user’s device and can be deleted by the user at any time.

3. Legal Basis (GDPR)

Since Kiro QR Scan does not collect personal data, no personal data processing occurs under Article 6 GDPR.

Camera access is based solely on user consent granted via Android system permissions.

Users may revoke permission at any time through device settings.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • The app does not use external servers
  • No cloud storage is used
  • No background data transmission occurs

All functionality operates locally on the device.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with:

  • Third parties
  • Advertising networks
  • Analytics providers
  • Government agencies

Unless required by applicable law.

6. International Data Transfers

No data is transferred outside the European Union, as no personal data is collected.

7. Children’s Privacy

The application is not specifically directed to children under 13.

However, since no personal data is collected, the app does not pose data collection risks to minors.

8. User Rights Under GDPR

Under GDPR, users have rights including:

  • Right of access
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability

Since no personal data is collected or stored, these rights generally do not apply in practice.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if application functionality changes.

The latest version will always be available in the app listing.
